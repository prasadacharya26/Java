Fizz

Appy Fizz is a product by Parle Agro, introduced in India in 2005.[1] Appy Fizz consists of carbonated apple juice, and can be used as the basis for cocktails. It does not contain alcohol.[2] Appy Fizz is also manufactured and marketed in Bangladesh by Global Beverage Co Ltd. under licence from Parle Agro.
